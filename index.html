We Are Agronet

AgroNet is a Digital Agriculture Platform that connects farmers, land owners, and investors, it is a platform where all the stakeholders involved pull their resources together to ensure increased food production and security in Africa. Farmers are assisted with funds, lands for cultivation, improved seeds, farm inputs, training on modern farming techniques and sales of their farm produce which gives them the capacity to farm more acres.

Our Farmers

In order to live, you need to eat, in order to eat, you need to farm, in order to farm you need to be a farmer. Farmers are the antidote to hunger. Agrotech provides a secure platform where farmers who can use the internet register their details, for those who cannot, we equally have a solution for that, we collect their datas manually and also sensitize them on what is required to work with Agronet. We also connect with farmers association, who provides us with a pool of datas on their members. From time to time, training and workshops are organized for farmers to keep them abreast with recent agricultural developments. By involving more people in agriculture, we can solve the problem of hunger and increase food production and security in Nigeria.

Our Investors

You can also be a part of our platform as an investor if you are interested in farming but do not have a land or are not available to work on the farm. You do this by putting in your money into our agricultural business which the farmers use for the farming processes and then you get a reasonable percentage of the profits made based on how much you invest. Our investors are individuals, business persons, government workers and anyone at all who has interest in farming and would be willing to contribute in cash even though they do not have lands and cannot farm.
